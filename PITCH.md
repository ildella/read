# Frankie Read

## Turn the web into your personal podcast.

You save articles you care about. You never read them. Everyone does.

Frankie Read closes the gap between **saving** and **consuming**. One click strips the noise from any web page and gives you a clean article you can read — or listen to, with a voice so natural it turns your reading backlog into a personal podcast.

---

## What it does

**Save.** Browser extension. One click. The article is extracted, cleaned from ads, popups, sidebars, invasive layouts, and stored locally on your device.

**Listen.** Modern Text-to-Speech turns any article into audio. Natural voices, long-form support, listening queue. Walk, commute, cook — your backlog becomes listening time.

**Understand.** Tap a word, a phrase, a passage you don't fully get. Frankie gives you a quick, contextual explanation inline. No new tabs, no detours, no breaking the flow. Especially useful when listening to dense or technical content.

---

## Why this, why now

- **Pocket is dead** (shut down 2025). Millions of users orphaned.
- **Dead time is real.** People walk, commute, do dishes. They want to consume content, not just bookmark it.
- **AI summarizers replace reading.** Frankie enables it. The goal isn't a shortcut — it's making the full article accessible in more moments of your day.
- **The web is louder than ever.** Readers need a calm, personal space.

---

## The core use case

The primary use case is **listening**.

Save a series of articles, hit play, go for a walk. Your personal podcast, made from the web you chose — not an algorithmic feed.

Reading is supported and beautiful. But the magic is audio.

---

## Design principle

**Calm over power.**

Frankie Read is deliberately small. No knowledge graphs, no AI agents, no social features, no complex automations. Clean reading, beautiful listening, a smart little assist when you're stuck.

The product ambition is not feature depth. It's **making you feel calm while finally consuming the articles you saved**.

---

## What it is not

Not a bookmark manager. Not a PKM. Not Readwise. Not Notion. Not an AI agent. Not a report generator. Not a paywall breaker.

---

## Technical identity

- **Offline-first.** Local storage, no mandatory cloud.
- **BYOK.** Bring your own TTS API key, or use ours. Open provider support (OpenAI, ElevenLabs, Gemini, local models).
- **PWA.** Progressive Web App. Works everywhere.
- **Small and sustainable.** Indie product, low operational costs.

---

## Business

Free tier for saving and reading. A few dollars a year for the full experience. BYOK keeps costs sustainable for power users and for us.

Not venture-scale. Built to last, built to stay small.
